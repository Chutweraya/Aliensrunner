Why I want to study about IT.

Often times I ask myself this very question, what’s my reason behind wanting to study the IT program? Without the assistant of technology, will my life be as simplistic as it is right now? Last year I was fortunate to study at the International Boarding School, India. Most of my education was computer-based. Some of the significances of Information and Technology (IT) are, it ease online shopping processes, and allows us to save money in order to communication with our loved ones, for instance, I and my parents, we talk a lot through the ease of LINE and MSN applications. My reason for returning to Thailand was the COVID-19 pandemic. Regardless, I tirelessly continued to study for my SATs and GEDs online, for a decent 3 months. In the midst of the virus, I could able to save plenty of time, transportation expenses, that’s why I find the IT program very attracting. Nonetheless, my family business was heavily disrupted by modern-trades companies that optimize the newest technologies, for instance, online applications and in-house management system. As a result, I acknowledged the limitless potential of IT, and want to explore more about what IT has to offer. One day, when I become and adept, I will be able to benefit Thailand hugely, as there are not many IT experts out there in the market. I want to transform my family’s brick and mortar business to a somewhat medium-big scale using BIG data, in which it will allow me to study customers’ behavior and analyze their tailored needs and wants. On top of that, produce a new platform that enhances cost-effective delivery and product quality in order to be more competitive. My long-term intention involves applying my knowledge to health and wellness sector, helping public health agency, patients and doctors to ease their operations in which will improve their productivity. Moreover, build a network the revolves around medical treatment to share the most updated methods to improve accuracy of treatments. As of now, using my IT skillsets, I am in the middle of assisting a doctor from the Department of Psychology, Faculty of Medicine, Khon Kaen University, proposing a project about Biofeedback for stress problems which lead to deadly situations. In addition to that, another long-term goal of mine is to create an ICT village for my city that provides top-notch knowledge for people of all ages, especially Boomer and Gen X to reduce disparity, technology-wise. The village will in a presence of personnel from the ICT fields, medical fields, healthcare fields, and economy fields. My ideal village will be the center of new IT that will enhance the quality of many lives. I believe it will be the center of attraction due to the credible personnel my village has to offer. At the end of the day, we are living in the upright age of IT and computing devices. I set-forth my goal to study specifically in this field to continue what my parents started, and I will certainly be impacting many lives in positive ways.
